How to - Generative Art

For this project, I made a piece of art that, when clicked, will spawn black bear heads on one side and brown bear heads on the other side

I first used an "if else" function to make it so that when I clicked, it made a bear appear where I clicked. I then used a a function I made titled "drawBear" to make a sort of function art with ellipses, which are used to make circles, and a line to make the mouth. Lastly, there's a small set of coding at the bottom. This is the main function that makes the bear drawn when the mouse is clicked, hence the "mouseClicked" at the beginning. The "(mouseX, mouseY)" makes it so that the function follows my mouse and can tell where I click.
